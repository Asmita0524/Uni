» Educational needs of the students

- Educational needs can also be termed as learners’ needs. These are the needs that every student has while they pursue any degree or education in general. A healthy classroom, good teachers, a good study routine, a good online teaching app, all these can be called as educational needs of the students. The different students may have different needs.
